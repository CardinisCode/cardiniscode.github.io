Background

The client's field service engineers were using paper-based gas safety assessment forms, which were time-consuming, prone to errors, and lacked integration with their existing systems. The manual process resulted in delayed reporting and inconsistent documentation.

Key Pain Points

  • Manual paper-based forms requiring physical storage and retrieval
  • Time-consuming data entry and report generation
  • Inconsistent formatting and potential for human error
  • No integration with existing CRM and customer management systems
  • Delayed customer communication and report delivery

Approach

Adopted a user-centered design approach, focusing on the needs of field service engineers while ensuring seamless integration with existing business processes.

User Research

Conducted interviews with field engineers to understand their workflow, pain points, and requirements for a digital solution. Observed actual assessment processes in the field to identify optimization opportunities.

Proposed Solution

Designed a mobile-first digital assessment system with automated report generation, email notifications, and Salesforce integration to streamline the entire process from field inspection to customer delivery.

Development

Implemented the solution using modern web technologies, ensuring mobile responsiveness and seamless integration with existing systems.

Lessons Learnt
